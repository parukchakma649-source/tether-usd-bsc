📌 What is Tether USD?

Tether USD is a stablecoin designed to maintain a 1:1 peg with the US Dollar. Built on BNB Smart Chain, it combines deep liquidity on PancakeSwap V3 with an autonomous stabilization engine that keeps the price within a tight band. The token serves as a reliable medium of exchange, store of value, and a safe haven during market volatility.

⚙️ How the peg is maintained

  • Concentrated Liquidity: The token is paired with USDT in a PancakeSwap V3 pool, concentrating liquidity around $1.
  • Autonomous Stabilizer: A dedicated stabilization engine monitors the price using TWAP and executes small corrective trades to keep it inside the 0.99‑1.02 band.
  • Inventory Protection: The system forces rebalancing if the treasury becomes too skewed toward one asset, preventing pool drainage.
  • Organic Activity: Multiple independent wallets perform randomized swaps, creating natural-looking volume and holder growth.
