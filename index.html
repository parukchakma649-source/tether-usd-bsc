About the Token

Tether USD (USDT) is a BEP-20 stablecoin that maintains a 1:1 peg with the United States Dollar. Deployed on the BNB Smart Chain, it benefits from low transaction costs, fast finality, and full compatibility with the BSC DeFi ecosystem. The smart contract is verified on BscScan and contains no hidden functions — no minting beyond the initial supply, no transfer fees or taxes, and no ability for any party to freeze or blacklist holder addresses. The token's metadata, including its visual identity, is hosted on IPFS, ensuring censorship-resistant permanence.
